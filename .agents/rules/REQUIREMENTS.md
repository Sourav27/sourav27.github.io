# Project: Personal Portfolio of Sourav Debnath
## Senior Product Manager (GenAI & Digital Transformation)

### 1. Overall Goal
To create a stunning, professional, and accessible personal portfolio website hosted on GitHub Pages. The site will serve as a dynamic resume and a digital showcase of Sourav's expertise in **AI Product Management**, **Digital Transformation**, and **User-Centric Design**. It aims to impress potential employers and expand professional networking opportunities while offering a personal touch for friends and family.

### 2. Core Identity & Theme
-   **Professional Identity:** Senior Product Manager shaping the future of AI.
-   **Design Aesthetic:** **Premium, Modern, High-Tech yet Human-Centric.**
    -   The design should reflect "shaping the future of AI" with sleek visuals, possibly dark mode, subtle animations, and a polished user interface.
    -   It must move beyond basic templates to a custom, high-quality look that demonstrates an eye for design and detail.
-   **Key Narrative:** A journey from Mechanical Engineering (IIT Madras) to General Management (IIM Bangalore) and leading 0-to-1 AI products at global scale (AB InBev, Vedanta).

### 3. Target Audience
-   **Primary:** Recruitment teams, Hiring Managers, and Professional Network (LinkedIn). Goal: Job offers, speaking engagements, networking.
-   **Secondary:** Friends and Family. Goal: Sharing personal passions (Photography, Art) and life updates.

### 4. Key Content Pillars
1.  **AI Product Leadership (The Core):**
    -   Highlight specific achievements: Scaling Data Insights Copilot to 500+ MAU, 10x growth, Agentic Workflows, high ROI.
    -   Showcase thought leadership in product management, AI and business impact.
2.  **Competence (Education & Skills):**
    -   Education: MBA from IIM Bangalore, B.Tech from IIT Madras.
    -   Skills: Product Leadership, roadmap strategy, Tech Stack (Azure, Python, React, SQL), and Collaboration tools.
3.  **Passions (The Human Side):**
    -   Hobbies: Photography, Art, and potentially blog posts derived from LinkedIn content.

### 5. Features Roadmap
-   **Landing Page:** High-impact Hero section introducing Sourav as a GenAI PM.
-   **Dynamic Resume:** Interactive display of experience (AB InBev, Bain, Vedanta) rather than a static PDF feel.
-   **Portfolio/Projects:** Case studies of AI projects and previous web dev work.
-   **Blog:** Section for professional thoughts, industry insights (synced/reposted from LinkedIn).
-   **Gallery:** A dedicated section for Photography and Art.
-   **Contact:** Clear calls to action for email, LinkedIn, GitHub and Instagram (for photography).

### 6. Technical Constraints
-   **Hosting:** GitHub Pages (Must be compatible with static site generation).
-   **Performance:** Fast loading, SEO optimized, and mobile-responsive.
-   **Maintenance:** Easy to update (markdown-based content preferred).

### 7. Success Metrics
-   "Wow" factor on first impression.
-   Clear communication of AI expertise.
-   Seamless navigation across professional and personal sections.
